# Positioning

## One-liner

A context-aware hype filter for AI agents.

## Tagline

This saves attention and bad decisions.

## Problem

AI agents summarize too easily and decide too rarely. They can turn every shiny repo, paper, product, or trend into a task.

## Audience

- founders with too many ideas
- consultants evaluating client tech
- developers choosing tools
- researchers triaging papers
- operators protecting team focus
- anyone with an overflowing backlog

## Differentiation

This is not a research agent. It is a decision layer before research becomes work.
